About this map

Inspired by the NYU Furman Center's interactive maps of city-owned land and the Mamdani administration's LIFT task force goal of 25,000 homes on public land.

Data: Department of City Planning City Owned and Leased Properties (COLP) joined to MapPLUTO for lot size, zoning and lot shapes, via NYC Open Data.

Full methodology, categories and caveats →

Made with AI assistance.
This map was built by joining public city datasets with an AI coding assistant. The categories are a simplification of a complex reality — a lot's true development potential depends on soil, easements, existing tenants, community plans and politics that no dataset fully captures. The 3D "capacity" is a rough zoning-based estimate, not a design. Treat everything here as a starting point for reporting, not a buildable-sites list. Verify any specific parcel against primary records before relying on it.
